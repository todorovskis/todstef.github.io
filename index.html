About me

I have travelled to many countries, experiencing their culture and history. Taking into account the high-cost of travelling i started a blog helping fellow explorers choose only the best accommodation, food and drinks. My blog has been received with warmth and kindness since the very beginning, giving me hope for the future and the things to come.

FROM

BITOLA, MACEDONIA

My current mission is to explore in depth my birth country, Macedonia. Known for its rich untouched nature and culture, with national parks such as Pelister and Galicica, it offers something for everyone. Born in Bitola, it has been the highlight of my life and the start of my exploring journey.

My Blog

Tour of Prague

May 24, 2018

One of those days you will remember for eternity. Incredible sightings in the town center. Street food is very good and affordable. The best was definitely the Tredelnik, a sweet pastry cone famous throught the Czech Republic.

The streets of Berlin

April 28, 2019

One of the great experiences of my life. Walking down the streets of Berlin, visiting the Berlin wall and remembering the dark history behind it. The afternoon was followed with a visit to the world-famous Alexanderplatz and the Berlin Metro.

The Eiffel Tower

August 16, 2019

Designed and built by the French civil engineer Gustave Alexandre, the Eiffel Tower is the landmark of Paris and France. To be able to see it up close was a very surreal thing.

Relaxing weekend

September 10, 2020

A 1.5h trip to Trpejca was one of the best decisions that week. Cristal clear water, incredible sunset and great people. What more does one need?

National Park Pelister

November 11, 2020

A cold morning turned into a fantastic afternoon spent in Nize Pole, a village in the National Park Pelister, on Baba Mountain. Fresh air and hot tea was all we needed to relax and enjoy the moment.
